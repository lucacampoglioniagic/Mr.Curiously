Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Mr. Curiously ("the App") is an automated content publishing tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or process any personal data from end users or third parties. It operates as a single-account automation tool owned and operated by its creator.

TikTok and Instagram Integration

The App uses OAuth tokens to publish content on behalf of a single, owner-controlled account. No user authentication flow is exposed to third parties. Tokens are stored securely as environment variables and never shared.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies.
