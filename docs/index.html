Mr. Curiously

Daily automated publisher of curious facts

About

Mr. Curiously is a fully automated application that publishes one curious fact every day about science, history, nature, and space. It generates AI-powered captions, branded images, and short-form videos, then publishes them automatically to Instagram and TikTok.
